The Ask Pipeline ГўВЂВ” New Partner Process
The ask begins in the fall. But the relationship that makes the ask possible is built year-round. Partnerships doesn't cold call ГўВЂВ” they build warm relationships first, understand what the partner cares about, then make a tailored ask that feels like a natural next step.
01

New partners come from three places: (1) companies that have served with E100 through community service ГўВЂВ” flagged by the CS Chair, (2) existing relationships held by the President or Exec Board, (3) companies identified through research that align with E100's mission. Partnerships Chair maintains the prospect list in Monday.com.
Owner: Partnerships Chair + Community Service Chair (on-ramp leads)

Research Their Goals First
Before any outreach, understand what the company cares about. What are their CSR priorities? DEI commitments? Community investment targets? Which of their KPIs can E100 help them hit? The pitch is not "here's what we do" ГўВЂВ” it's "here's how we help you do what you care about."
