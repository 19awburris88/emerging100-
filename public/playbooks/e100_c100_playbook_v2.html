Institutional Knowledge
North Star: Build leaders who go back and serve. C100 students don't just develop themselves — they graduate committed to mentoring the next generation. BEST → C100 → Professional → Mentor. This is the cycle.
RISE is a flagship, not a footnote: RISE is an 8-month leadership development program, application-based, all women. Gas South sponsors the graduation in March. It runs on its own arc — not a sub-event of C100's general programming. Treat it accordingly.
Communication flows through chapter leadership: E100 doesn't go to campuses. Info gets to students through the Chapter President & VP via the assigned E100 campus liaison. A weak liaison = a dark campus.
Career Fair is always Fall: Melvia Jones at Delta Air Lines is the key contact. Book early. All 5 campuses mobilized through their liaisons.
BEST Academy vs. Collegiate 100 — The Difference
Same pipeline, different stages. BEST grows boys into men. C100 grows college students into servant leaders who mentor others. The tone, content, and relationships must reflect where students are in their journey.
B.E.S.T. Academy
  • ›High school — grades 6–12
  • ›Focus: GRIT, Identity, Confidence, Leadership, Accountability
  • ›E100 goes TO the school — on-campus, in the library
  • ›Monthly sessions, 3rd Friday — highly structured
  • ›Culminates in Rites of Passage — ceremonial, symbolic
  • ›Adult mentors guiding youth — character development tone
Collegiate 100
  • ›College students — 5 Atlanta campuses
  • ›Focus: Career readiness, self-leadership, EQ, servant leadership
  • ›Students come TO E100 events — off-campus
  • ›8 distinct initiatives across the year — event-driven
  • ›Culminates in EOY BBQ — celebratory, relational
  • ›Peer-to-professional mentorship — aspirational, not directive
The pipeline: BEST student → graduates → enters college → joins C100 → career-ready leader → graduates → becomes E100 member or community mentor. Every stage feeds the next.

All C100 Initiatives — Full List
C100 is event-driven. Unlike BEST's monthly cadence, C100 revolves around 8 distinct initiatives across the year. Each is its own mini-project with its own planning timeline, budget, and stakeholders.
C100-01 · Flagship Program
The RISE Leadership Experience
8-Month Program

An 8-month women's leadership development program. Application-based — students apply to participate. Runs its own arc from fall through graduation in March. Gas South sponsors the graduation. Past speakers involved. This is a flagship initiative that deserves its own full planning treatment.

Program Structure
  • Application-based — women apply to be selected
  • 8 months of structured programming
  • Leadership identity, confidence, voice development
  • EQ & emotional resilience for women leaders
  • Community service leadership component
  • Cross-campus (all 5 campuses eligible)
  • Past speakers involved — build speaker pipeline
Key Milestones
  • Fall — Applications open & cohort selected
  • Monthly programming sessions (format TBD with Chairs)
  • March — RISE Graduation · Sponsored by Gas South
  • Pre/post survey — measure confidence, leadership identity
Partners Needed

  • Women-focused corporate partners TBD

New Chair Questions — What Would Block You
Every question a brand new C100 Chair would need answered. Red = planning blocker. Amber = go ask your board liaison. Blue = good to know.
🔴 Blocker
Which swim lane am I responsible for? Campus Liaisons & Programming, Workforce Dev & Career Fair, or RISE Program? I need to know my lane before I can plan a single event.
🔴 Blocker
Who are the current C100 student leaders at each campus? Who is the chapter president or VP at each of the 5 campuses? Without these contacts I cannot coordinate anything at the campus level.
🔴 Blocker
What events are locked vs. what do I plan from scratch? Career Fair at Delta HQ is confirmed annually — but when exactly? What else is on the calendar and what's TBD for this year?
🔴 Blocker
What companies are invited to Career Fair and who reaches out to them? Do I contact companies directly or does Partnerships handle outreach? What is the company list from last year?
🔴 Blocker
What is the RISE program? Is there a curriculum document? Who facilitates — E100 members, external facilitators, or corporate partners like Gas South? I cannot run an 8-month program without a framework.
🔴 Blocker
What is my budget and how do I access it? C100 spans 5 campuses with multiple events. What is the annual budget and how do I submit expenses — pre-approval or reimbursement?
📋 Board
Who is my primary board liaison — Daniel Farr or Julius Thomas? Both are listed. Which one do I call first and how often do we connect?
📋 Board
National Mentorship Month event — what is the format this year? Top Golf is not confirmed. What is the plan, budget, and who is the target audience — C100 students or E100 members?
📋 Board
The mental health event format changes each year — what is the direction for 2026-27? A RISE-themed cohort for young men was discussed. Who decides the format and who facilitates?
📋 Board
How does the C100-to-E100 pipeline work from my end? When I identify graduating seniors worth engaging, what is my role? Do I flag them to ED Chair directly?
