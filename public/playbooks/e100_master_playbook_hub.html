The Mission
"The Emerging 100 of Atlanta serves as role models in the community,
helping grow the next leadership ecosystem."
Every program, every event, every dollar raised, every hour volunteered exists in service of this mission. The classroom to the college campus to the corporation — that is the pipeline E100 builds, funds, and walks alongside.

3rd Friday Monthly

30th or 31st Monthly
