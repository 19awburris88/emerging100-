Institutional Knowledge
North Star: Each session must connect to a cohesive annual leadership arc 芒聙聰 not feel like standalone events. The Chair owns the narrative. The Facilitator chair ensures partner topics ladder into it.
The Tie is the Symbol: The Rites of Passage "passing of the dress tie" is the emotional core of the entire program. Document it every year. Photograph it. This is the moment that resonates with donors, families, and the community.
Mikal Driver is a bridge: Dual role as E100 member AND B.E.S.T. Academy employee. He is the most valuable internal connector to the school. Protect and leverage this relationship 芒聙聰 he is Consulted on all school-facing decisions.
Pre-Survey is mandatory: BEST and C100 are the two programs that require pre/post student surveys. This gives the org measurable impact data for funders and helps the content team meet students where they are.
Food is not optional: Every session requires food 芒聙聰 catered or sponsored. It signals respect to the students. Operations chair maintains the preferred vendor list and an active food sponsor pipeline.
