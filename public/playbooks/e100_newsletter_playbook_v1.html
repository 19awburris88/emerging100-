Distribution Lists
Two lists. Both must be maintained and grown every year. At the end of every event √¢¬Ä¬î every Social Fundraiser, every Gala, every program event, every GBM √¢¬Ä¬î collect attendee emails and add them to both lists. This is how the lists grow.
List 1 √¢¬Ä¬î Newsletter
Monthly Email Newsletter
Receives the full monthly issue every 30th/31st. Includes: all E100 members, all corporate partners and sponsors, community stakeholders, event attendees who opt in, and anyone who has been in the room with E100.
List 2 √¢¬Ä¬î Social Blast
Event + Ticket Announcements
Receives targeted blasts when events go on sale, Gala tables open, and other time-sensitive announcements. Broader than the newsletter list √¢¬Ä¬î includes anyone who has attended any E100 Social Fundraiser or public event.
Annual List Refresh Process
Every year, go through every event of the entire year and dump all attendees into both lists. This is the primary list-building mechanism. Every Social Fundraiser, every Gala, every GBM, every community service event, every H&W event √¢¬Ä¬î collect the emails and add them. The Newsletter Chair owns this process.
01
Collect attendee lists from every event
After every event √¢¬Ä¬î Social Fundraisers (Ticket Falcon export), Gala (Ticket Falcon export), GBMs (sign-in sheet), community service events (volunteer sign-in), H&W events (registration or sign-in). Newsletter Chair is responsible for requesting these lists from each committee Chair within 1 week of each event.
02

Remove duplicates, correct obvious errors, remove any opt-outs. The list should be clean and ready to send before the next issue goes out.
03

Gala and Social Fundraiser attendees √¢¬Ü¬í both lists. GBM attendees √¢¬Ü¬í Newsletter list. Community service volunteers √¢¬Ü¬í Newsletter list. Partners and sponsors √¢¬Ü¬í both lists. E100 members √¢¬Ü¬í both lists.
04
Major annual refresh √¢¬Ä¬î June
At the end of the program year (after the May issue), do a full annual dump of all event attendees from the entire year into both lists. This is the biggest list refresh of the year √¢¬Ä¬î do it before the new Chairs take over in June so the incoming Newsletter Chair inherits a current, clean list.
