Does Finance own dues tracking or does P&G own compliance on dues?

What financial reports, if any, does Finance produce and who receives them?
What tools does Finance use — QuickBooks, spreadsheets, Monday.com?
What is the relationship between Finance and Ticket Falcon payouts from Social Fundraisers?
